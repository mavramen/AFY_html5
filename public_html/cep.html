Participating in International Cultural and Educational Exchange programmes could be a life changing experience. It now only brodens students point of view but also provides opportunities to gain new skills and knowledge. Programmes have an aim of helping to increase the participants' understanding and tolerance of other cultures, as well as improving their language skills and broadening their social horizons.
